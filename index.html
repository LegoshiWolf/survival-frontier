About the Game

Survival Frontier is an open-world survival game where players must explore a hostile environment, build shelters, scavenge for resources, and fight off threats to survive. Test your survival skills in a vast, dynamic world filled with danger and opportunities.

Game Features

Open World

Explore a vast and diverse open world with different biomes and challenges.


Survival Mechanics

Manage hunger, thirst, health, and more as you fight to survive.

Crafting System

Build shelters, tools, and weapons from available resources.

Loot and Scavenging

Search for valuable items and resources to aid your survival.

Day-Night Cycle

Adapt to the changing environment as day turns to night.

Enemy AI

Face off against hostile creatures and NPCs with varying behaviors.
